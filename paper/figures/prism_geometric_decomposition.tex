\caption{\textbf{PRISM decomposition of the risk gap.} The cross-entropy risk gap
$|\mathcal{R}_T - \mathcal{R}_P|$ between target $T$ and proxy $P$ splits,
under Procrustes alignment $W^{*}{=}\arg\min_{W\in\mathcal{O}(d)}\|Z_T - Z_PW\|_F$,
into a feature alignment error $\delta$ and a head discrepancy $\gamma$
(Thm.~\ref{thm:unified_bound}). $\delta$ further decomposes exactly into
\emph{scale mismatch} $(\rho_T{-}\rho_P)^{2}$ and \emph{shape mismatch}
$2\rho_T\rho_P(1{-}\Omega)$ (Prop.~\ref{prop:exact_decomposition});
$\gamma$ measures the head offset $\Delta H{=}W^{*}H_T{-}H_P$ re-weighted by
the proxy's active subspace $\Sigma_P$, and vanishes exactly when $H_T{=}H_P$
(frozen-head LoRA).}
