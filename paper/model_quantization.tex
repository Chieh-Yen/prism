\paragraph{Missing combinations.}
The five missing entries (3 models $\times$ GPTQ/AWQ) share a common pattern: they are all \emph{base} (pre-trained) models for which the community quantization ecosystem is less active than for their instruct-tuned counterparts.
Specifically:
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
    \item \textbf{Qwen3-8B-Base} --- no AWQ checkpoint; the official \texttt{Qwen/Qwen3-8B-AWQ} is quantized from the instruct variant.
    \item \textbf{Ministral-3-8B-Base-2512} --- no GPTQ or AWQ checkpoint; only instruct and reasoning variants have community quants.
    \item \textbf{Ministral-3-8B-Instruct-2512} --- no GPTQ checkpoint; AWQ is available via \texttt{cyankiwi}.
    \item \textbf{Qwen2.5-7B} --- no GPTQ or AWQ checkpoint for the base model; official quantizations are published only for the instruct variant.
\end{itemize}
For these models, GGUF (6 bit-widths) and BitsAndBytes (3 configurations) still provide comprehensive coverage across the 2--8 bit range.
